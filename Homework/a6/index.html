Tokyo is the largest city in the world and the capital of Japan. Tokyo originally had the name Edo when it was first founded in 1603 by Shogun Tokugawa Ieyasu. Today, Tokyo is a bustling metropolis full of industry and commerce, while remaining attractive for tourists due to its high-tech nature and aesthetic appeal.
