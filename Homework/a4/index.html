Sofia Gubaidulina's Bassoon Repertoire

Sofia Gubaidulina is a Russian composer who was born in the year 1931. Gubaidulina's music is characterized by her frequent use of extended instrumental techniques, such as multiphonics and microtones. Currently, Sofia Gubaidulina has composed two primary works for bassoon, her Concerto for bassoon and low strings (1975) and her Duo sonata for two bassoons (1977).
